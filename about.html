About Me

The story behind the data scientist

Hi, I'm Rishabh

I'm a computational researcher at the University of Victoria, working at the intersection of data science and healthcare. My work focuses on understanding how the immune system responds to cancer, using computational models to simulate biological processes that could lead to better treatments.

With a double major in Computer Science and Statistics, I bridge the gap between complex algorithms and real-world medical applications. I believe that the most impactful work happens when technical expertise meets genuine curiosity about solving human problems.

When I'm not analyzing tumor microenvironments or training machine learning models, you'll find me exploring Victoria's trails, experimenting with new technologies, or diving into research papers that push the boundaries of what's possible.
